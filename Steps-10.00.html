Why is Project Reference Important?

The development and evaluation of our predictive models for this case study were informed by a variety of sources. These include historical sales data from the Global Superstore, a retail company specializing in a diverse range of products, which provided a comprehensive view of sales performance across various product categories and markets. The dataset, sourced from Data.World, consists of over 50,000 records covering sales, profit, categories, subcategories, and regions. The model was trained on this data, learning to recognize and apply inherent trends to future predictions.
