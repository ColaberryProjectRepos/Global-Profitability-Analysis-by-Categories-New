Establish Calendar Relationship 12
  1. Click on the following icon linkto open the video tutorial on how to establish a calendar relationship and retrieve the data using a DAX formula. 
  2. Navigate to Modeling Tab: Go to the ‘Modeling’ tab and click on ‘New Table’.
  3. Enter DAX Formula: Type the required DAX formula into the DAX formula bar.

    Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS (
        CALENDAR (MIN('Orders'[order_date]), MAX('Orders'[order_date])),
        "DateAsInteger", FORMAT ([Date], "YYYYMMDD" ),
        "Year", YEAR ( [Date] ),
        "Monthnumber", FORMAT ( [Date], "MM" ),
        "YearMonthnumber", FORMAT ( [Date], "YYYY/MM" ),
        "YearMonthShort", FORMAT ( [Date], "YYYY/mmm" ),
        "MonthNameShort", FORMAT ( [Date], "mmm" ),
        "MonthNumberForSorting", MONTH([Date]),
        "Quarter", "Q" & FORMAT ( [Date], "Q" ),
        "YearQuarter", FORMAT ( [Date], "YYYY" ) & "/Q" & FORMAT ( [Date], "Q" )
    )

    1. Save Changes: Confirm the formula by clicking on the check mark.
    2. Create Relationship: Navigate to the ‘Diagram View’ in Power BI Desktop.
    3. Connect Columns: Drag and connect the ‘Order_Date’ column of the ‘Orders’ table to the ‘Date’ column of the ‘Calendar’ table.
    4. Confirm Relationship: A line will appear connecting the two tables, indicating a relationship has been established.
