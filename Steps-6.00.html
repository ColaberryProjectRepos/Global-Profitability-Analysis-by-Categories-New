Case 1 Objective: Optimizing Profit in High-Performing Categories

Positive Insight:
Analysis reveals that the Technology category has seen a 30% increase in Sales Year over Year  across the board, particularly in Germany, which leads the profit margin increase among countries.



Negative Insight: 

However, the Furniture category experienced a significant 53% decline in gross profit margins in markets like Denmark, necessitating strategic reassessment.



Impact

Solution Impact:
ROI Calculation: By reallocating resources towards promoting Technology sales in high-margin countries like Germany and reducing exposure in low-performing areas, a hypothetical 20% increase in net profits could be achieved based on industry averages. Assuming an annual profit baseline of $2 million in these categories, this strategy could yield an additional $400,000 in profits.

Investment: $50,000
Additional Profit: $20,000
ROI: 40%
Lower initial investment of $50,000
Generates an additional profit of $20,000

Offers a 40% return on investment, meaning for every dollar spent, you gain an extra $0.40 in profit. Suitable for those looking for a cost-effective option with less initial investment.
