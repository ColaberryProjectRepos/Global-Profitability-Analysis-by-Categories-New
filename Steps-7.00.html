Case Studies Case 2 7

Objective: Expand Market Share in Emerging Markets

Positive Insight:

Emerging markets, such as India, show a 25%  increase in Sales Year over Year for the Office Supplies category, indicating untapped potential and growing demand.



Negative Insight:
High logistic costs in Brazil have led to a -9% GPM year over year profit decline in the same category (Office Supplies), suggesting operational inefficiencies.



Impact

Solution Impact:
ROI Calculation: Focusing on streamlining operations in Brazil while aggressively targeting the Indian market for Office Supplies could lead to a 15% net profit increase. With an assumed annual profit of $1.5 million in these areas, the strategy might result in an additional $225,000 in profits.
Investment: $150,000
Cost Savings: $200,000
ROI: Approximately 133%
Requires a higher initial investment of $150,000
Results in cost savings of $200,000

Provides an ROI of approximately 133%, meaning for every dollar spent, you save roughly $1.33 in operational costs.
A strong choice for those prioritizing long-term operational efficiency, even though it involves a larger upfront investment.
